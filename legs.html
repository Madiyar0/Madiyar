Exercises for legs

Let's start pumping our chest and triceps!

Squats with a barbell on the back

Take the barbell from the racks, bring your shoulder blades together, straighten your chest, straighten your back. Put your feet shoulder-width apart, slightly unfold your socks to the sides. Pull the pelvis back and sit down to parallel the hips with the floor.

Squats with a barbell on the chest

Remove the barbell from the racks, bring your elbows forward, bend your back. Put your feet shoulder-width apart, slightly unfold your socks to the sides. Sit down to the parallel of the hips with the floor or slightly lower and rise back up to the parallel.

Lunges

If you find it difficult to keep your balance in this exercise, try back thrusts: they are easier, but less effective.You can make lunges with different weights: with a barbell on your shoulders, with dumbbells in your hands, with an elastic band-expander. To begin with, it's better to try dumbbells: it will be.

Leg press in the simulator

The same as squats, but with minimal load on the muscles of the core and extensors of the back. Due to this, you can take more weight without risking tearing off your back, and it is better to load your hips. In addition, the leg press pumps the front part of the thigh as.

Deadlift

The use of a curved barbell increases the involvement of the biceps in work, since with a slight turn of the palm, its inner part is more actively involved in movement. The result of performing such a variation of the exercise is a more voluminous and "spherical" biceps.
