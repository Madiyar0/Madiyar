Chest and Triceps exercises

Let's start pumping our chest and triceps!

Push-ups

This exercise is much more difficult than classical push-ups, it provides a serious load on the pectoral muscles, but requires training and arm strength.

Push-ups on the uneven

For this exercise, you will need bars. Such complexes are sold with a horizontal bar and bars for the house, on which you can perform a lot of exercises.

Bench press dumbbells

Lie down on a bench or on the floor, if you study at home, and take dumbbells in your hands so that the palms are facing each other.

Reverse push-ups from the bench

Starting position - heels touch the floor, hands on the bench behind your back. Slowly lower the torso down, stay at the bottom point, then with explosive force push the body weight up over the bench.

French bench press

The mechanics of the exercise consists in slowly lifting the weight from behind the head - due to the conscious involvement of the triceps. The exercise is usually performed with a curved EZ-barbell, as it helps to keep the elbows.

Triceps extension in a tilt

Exercise for pumping the long and lateral heads of the triceps. When pulling the arm back, make sure that the elbow does not change position (that is, do not swing the arm back and forth or left and right).
